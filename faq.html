FAQ



New prospective members can join by attending one of our mass meetings, held at the beginning of each semester. Information about mass meeting dates/times can be found on the HackBlue website once they are scheduled. We can also be found at Festifall and Winterfest, or reached through our Contact page.

In order to maintain membership with HackBlue, one must be an active member of at least one committee, as well as attend our biweekly meetings. There is no membership fee.

No computer science experience is required to be a HackBlue member!
