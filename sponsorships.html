Sponsorships

We are seeking sponsorship to help realize our mission of increasing accessibility to education in technology. Whether you are a small business or an industry leader, partnering with HackBlue means:

• A unique opportunity to support our mission of democratizing technology education

• Directly impacting learning opportunities for underrepresented minorities to break into the technology industry

• Brand exposure through social media and team apparel

• Direct recruiting channels for our talented team of computer scientists and a foothold in the University of Michigan campus

Please email hackblue-leadership@umich.edu if you are interested in supporting our mission.

Gifts in kind and professional mentorship are also greatly appreciated and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
